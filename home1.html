Kota Bahru, Kelantan

Growing up in Kelantan, I have a deep appreciation for Malaysia's diverse customs and culture. I bring a strong appreciation for legacy, community, and perseverance from my hometown to all I do.

There are several sites in Kelantan that showcase its tradition and attraction. My hometown has many natural wonders, from the serene beaches of Pantai Cahaya Bulan to the adventurous trails and waterfalls of Gunung Stong. Its vibrant Siti Khadijah Market is a center of culture where traditional crafts like batik-making and songket weaving coexist with regional specialties like nasi kerabu, nasi dagang, and keropok lekor. My appreciation for cultural variety has grown as a result of these experiences, which have also improved my ability to be hospitable, flexible, and cooperative. My enthusiasm for engaging with people and fostering cultural heritage was further stoked by my participation in neighborhood gatherings like the Kelantan International Kite Festival.

I have a great feeling of pride in my heritage and a dedication to upholding cultural customs because of being raised in Kelantan. These principles still influence my career and personal development, motivating me to make a significant contribution to any group or community I'm a part of.
